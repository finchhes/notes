# the basics
hockey, specifically ice hockey in this is a physical / contact sport played on ice. players use sticks made of carbon fiber, kevlar, and fiberglass (previously wood) to hit rubber pucks along the rink and into the net on the opposing teams side. im going to be mostly focusing on nhl rules and standards because its the easiest, different leagues have different rules.
