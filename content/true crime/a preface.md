### i dont "condone".
crazy that i have to say this

i am interested in a lot of true crime cases, many including violence perpetrated by children. this is a sensitive subject and i try to handle it with the most respect i can. my heart goes out to the victims, their families, and all who were affected.

i specifically do not identify with
- the "tcc" or true crime community at large
- "columbiners" or whatever theyre calling themselves now

or any other group that romanticizes these events

mostly because theyre actually lame as fuck and care way too much about seeming edgy and cool when in reality they are the biggest losers on earth

### that being said...
i will not try to moralize or justify my interest in these events. i think people who solely talk about the psychology behind it are kidding themselves, because beyond all that it really is just an instinct to learn. a curiosity

i have mixed emotions on [[Eric Harris|eric]] and [[Dylan Klebold|dylan]], as do most. i hold a sort of sympathy for them, even if they do not deserve it. i hope they find peace in death and i'm sorry that the world failed them, but that does not take away towards my hurt and sympathy for their victims whos lives were taken too soon.
