- sender
- receiver
- message  
    - the thoughts and reality that the sender sends to the receiver
        - i want to ask somebody someone, im the sender theyre the receiver
- channels 
    - verbal communication
    - text / electronic communication
    - nonverbal communication
- noise
    - limitations to the channel 
        - internal noise
            - miscommunication
            - thoughts and distractions
        - real world noise
- feedback
    - response and reaction to information
        - tones, emotions, sarcasm
        - a lot of nonverbal communication
    - sender becomes receiver 
- environment/context/situation
    - external
        - where it takes place, when
        - make it a good place to say what you need to
            - what does the situation need
- frame of reference/field of experience
    - life experience, culture
        - beliefs and values
