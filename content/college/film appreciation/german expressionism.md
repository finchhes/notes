## historic context
- post war Germany 
    - between 2 world wars
- fine art, theatre, film

## what it is
- highly stylised visuals reflecting the psychological state of humans
- depiction of reality distorted for emotional affect
- experimental art with their specific mediums

## characteristics
- modernity
    - beautiful
    - dehumanising
- fascination with supernatural
- themes of
    - insecurity
    - betrayal
    - abuse of power
    - distorted sense of reality
    - religion
    - existentialism
- chiaroscuro lighting
    - extreme contrast
- non-realistic and odd geometric sets
- over exaggerated acting
- spectacular settings
- strong visual elements

## contributions
- created film noir
- science fiction
- horror
