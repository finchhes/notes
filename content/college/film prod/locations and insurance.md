- location manager
    - scouting can be done by a producer but mostly falls under a location manager and their team 
    - a location managers job includes 
        - research possible locations based on script 
        - negotiate contracts and secure any needed location permits 
        - make sure the locations are clean and report any damages before shoot
- tech scouts 
    - once locations are secured department heads will come and look at the location, make their own plans and figure out what equipment will work best for the location 
        - dp and lighting 
        - sound department 
        - art department 

# tips 
- bring camera and take pictures 
- try to scout at the time youd be shooting 
- identifying staging areas for cast and crew
- locate bathrooms 
- ask about parking 
- check power supplies 
- be direct with the property owner about the shoot
