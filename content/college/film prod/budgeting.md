# budgeting and accounting
- the budget is the detailed plan and list of total expenses needed to make the film
- the accountant and accounting department is responsible for managing the productions bank accounts

# production accountant
- meet with producers regarding budget 
- track cost 
- oversee payroll for cast and crew

## preparing the budget
- in the development phase, a preliminary budget is made. its the estimated cost of production based on the script itself 
- the redefined budget is made and prepared once the script breakdown is done and becomes more accurate one the shooting schedule is made

## making the budget 
- every budget starts off with the top sheet or a detailed summary of all the expenses listed and it shows the total of the whole production at the top 
- next part is a breakdown of each category of expenses

### the categories
- above the line
    - writer 
    - producer
    - director
    - actors
- below the line 
    - rest of crew (dp, ad, sound dep)
    - extras
    - production equipment
    - props/vehicles
    - locations
- post production 
    - editor
    - vfx
    - sound studios
    - color correctionist
    - editing suites
    - equipment/software
- other expenses
    - marketing
    - distribution
    - film festivals
    - production office expenses

### things to remember
- food. you need to feed people
- what kind of production 
- factor in any union the crew is in 
- budget can change
- contingency plan (10-15%)
