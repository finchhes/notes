# mise-en-scene
french for setting the scene
all the visual arrangements in a frame and in front of the camera
- parts of mise-en-scene
    - lights
        - colour
    - production design
    - costumes
    - hair and makeup
    - blocking
    - composition

## shots
- moment of action between when a camera starts and stops rolling
- building blocks of a scene
- types of shots
    - extreme wide 
        - no subject
    - very wide
        - maybe subject, very far away
    - wide
        - full body
    - medium wide
        - knee up
    - medium
        - waist up
    - medium close up
        - chest up
    - close up
        - shoulder up
    - big close up
        - facial
    - extreme close up
        - one part of the face
    - cut in
## angles
- high angle
- low angle
- two shot
- over the shoulder

## movement
- panning
- tilting
- push in (dolly)
- zoom
- tracking
