# the casting process
the process in which you choose actors for TV, stage, and film.
- happens in the pre production phase 
    - creative side
- director leads casting process with a casting director or producer if needed

## how productions cast actors
- contact an actors agent directly with info of the film and character 
- productions put out a casting call to casting agencies and other casting sites and actors go through the audition process

## casting call 
- a notice by a production looking for actors in specific roles for a film and/or other production
- casting call includes 
    - name and short summary of the production 
    - description of the roles 
    - time frame of the production
    - location 
### tips 
- be descriptive 
- be specific in what you're looking for 
    - age, race, gender 
    - specific skills 
- anything the actor should know?
    - is it explicit?
    - union or non - union 
    - paid or non - paid
