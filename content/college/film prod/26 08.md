## the five stages of filmmaking
- development
    - where brainstorming starts
        - logline
        - conflict
        - characters
    - key crew is identified
    - budget is made
        - what will go into the set
        - paying crew and cast
    - story is pitched to executives
        - get funding
        - "how flexible are you"
- pre production
    - planning phase
        - casting actors
        - finding locations
        - finalising budget
    - rest of the crew 
    - creative and management 
        - creative
            - shooting script
            - works with dp 
            - rehearsing with actors
        - management
            - logistics
                - securing sets
                - scheduling
                - getting permits
                - making sure everyone is happy
    - main crew
        - director
        - producer
        - dp 
        - assistant director
            - "running the show" on set
        - sound department
    - other crew
        - production assistant
            - lowkey abused
        - script supervisor 
            - everything is consistant
                - lighting
                - positions
                - where are they holding things
        - grip
            - setting everything up
        - gaffer    
            - setting up the lights
            - works under dp
        - assistant camera
            - working under dp 
            - slate reports
        - production designer
            - dressing the set
            - creating the set 
- production
    - shoot everything
- post production
    - editing and putting it together
        - multiple rounds
        - getting notes on the edit 
            -
        - stages
            - dailies
            - assembly cut
                - looking over things
                - going through everything
                - string it all together
                    - no cuts
            - rough cut
                - making a rough draft of the film
                - getting lots of notes
            - fine cut
                - cut thats the closest to the final cut
                - picture lock
                    - done cutting
                    - focusing on colour and sound
            - final cut
                - completed cut
- exhibition & distribution
    - finished film
        - screening
            - film festivals
            - online
        - distribution companies to stream it
